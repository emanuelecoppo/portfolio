ZEPTEPI2020

Illustration.

Client

ZEPTEPI, a musical project based in Bergamo, Italy.

Concept

Graphic project for the musical group ZEPTEPI. It includes a main logo and a series of symbols. The visual identity of the band is inspired by the ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs, revisited in a modern and futuristic key. Each one is linked to an emotion or an aspect of human life. Each symbol matches a specific letter: together they form an artificial alphabet and a new writing system.
