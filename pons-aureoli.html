Pons Aureoli2019

Brand Identity, Web Design.

Client

Pons Aureoli S.r.l., dental office based in Canonica d’Adda, BG, Italy.

Concept

Brand identity for the dental office Pons Aureoli. The logo, built on the golden spiral, represents the “Pons Aureoli” (Latin for “golden bridge”), an historic bridge in the city of Canonica d’Adda, from which the studio took the name. The project includes the development of a fully responsive website.
