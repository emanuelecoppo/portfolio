Brief

Designing the cover of a book.

Concept

A new book jacket for the classic adventure by Jules Verne, Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea, imagined as a part of the Penguin Classics series. The illustration is inspired by some of the most significant scenes of the story: the deep-sea diver is trying to grab a shiny treasure (represented by the book’s title). On the back, there is a quote from the book.
